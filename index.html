Step Into Shared Experience

If a stranger traveled to the heart of your experience, how long would they need to walk before they found themselves?

Through the Threshold is a space to step into the stories, myths, and reflections that hold us. Here, art, philosophy, reflection and mysticism become tools.

The release of your experience will not come through understanding alone, but through offering it as a bridge to someone else's life.
